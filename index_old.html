About

I am currently a PhD Student at the Computer Science department of University of Liverpool. My research focus is on improving methods for structured prediction, transfer learning and multi-task learning in natural language. I'm apart of the Data Mining and Machine Learning (DMML) Research Group and the Natural Language Processing Group.

Background
I studied Electronic Engineering at the National University of Ireland, Maynooth (NUIM) in Co.Kildare from Ireland, where I am originally from. Motivated by undergraduate final year project, I began a MSc in Computer Science (Major in Data Analytics) at the National University of Ireland, Galway (NUIG). I then worked as a Research Assistant in the Unit for Natural Language Processing (UNLP) at the Insight Centre for Data Analytics.
